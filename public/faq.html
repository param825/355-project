How do I start a garden from scratch?

Starting a garden from scratch involves several steps: choose a location, test the soil, prepare the soil, select the right plants, and plant them properly. Make sure to water and maintain your garden regularly.

What are the best plants for my climate?

The best plants for your climate depend on your local hardiness zone. Research plants that thrive in your specific zone to ensure they can withstand the weather conditions.

How can I deal with common pests organically?

To deal with common pests organically, you can use methods such as companion planting, introducing beneficial insects, and using natural pest control products like neem oil or diatomaceous earth.

What services do you offer, and how can I book them?

We offer a variety of services including lawn care, landscaping, garden maintenance, and custom projects. You can book our services by contacting us via phone, email, or through our website's contact form.
